The pomegranate is a fruit that contains hundreds of edible seeds called arils. They are rich in fiber, vitamins, minerals and bioactive plant compounds, but they also contain some sugar. It can be used in fruit salad or to make juice. It is also used in custard. It is rich in vitamin C, K , folate and potassium and thus have various health benefits. The punicalagins in pomegranate juice have been shown to reduce inflammation, one of the leading drivers of many serious diseases.
